Security and Privacy in 6G Networks

New Areas and New Challenges

The limitations of 5G networks are gradually being discovered as 5G networks are deployed. These limitations have driven exploratory research into 6G networks. Although research into the development of 6G networks already exists, the fundamental security and privacy problems of 6G networks are still under investigation. In this paper, we first review history of previous network technologies in order to illuminate the trends informing 6G networking. We then identify the four key areas in 6G networks and survey some promising associated key technologies, along with the related security and privacy issues. The applications of the 6G network are then presented. The intention of this article is to survey the new areas in 6G networking, along with their security and privacy challenges, in order to lay the foundation for future 6G network-related security and privacy research.
